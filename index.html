QB Captain Showdown Dashboard

A synthesis tool for high volume DraftKings NFL Showdown players. Filter first place QB Captain lineups to see ownership, salary left, and construction patterns.

Filters

How to use this dashboard

This dataset includes first place DraftKings NFL Showdown winning lineups where the Captain is a QB. Use filters to isolate a team, season, or ownership range, then compare how winners were built.

  • Use ownership and salary left to understand how “popular” or “different” QB Captain winners were.
  • Use stack depth to see how often a QB Captain needed one, two, or more pass catchers from his team.
  • Turn patterns into rules you can test across a portfolio rather than reacting to one slate.

Data notes

  • This shows patterns inside QB Captain winners only.
  • It does not measure how often QB Captain appears across the full contest field.
  • Use it for decision support and review, not as a guarantee.
